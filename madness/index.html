Preface

I find it best to not shy away from uncomfortable conversations, especially if the future is in question. I have never been good at putting a filter on my words. This will be the one rule behind my "Midnight Madness" series, designed to be written late at night when my thoughts are at their rawest form. I will be unapologetic.
