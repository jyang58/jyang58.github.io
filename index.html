About

Hey! I guess welcome to the page. I started this website one restless night at 3 in the morning after I spent the whole day coding... maybe I guess I just couldn't get enough? As I laid awake in the darkness just staring at the ceiling, I looked torwards the future and saw nothing. Absolutely nothing. I had no clue what lay ahead, and as someone who finds comfort through knowledge, this lack of foresight disturbed me greatly. Where could I find answers though? I realized that the questions about my future could only be answered by myself. What I do is ultimately decided by me.
