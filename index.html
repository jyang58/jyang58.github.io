Midnight Madness

I find it best to not shy away from uncomfortable conversations, especially if the future is in question. I have never been good at putting a filter on my words. This will be the one rule behind my "Midnight Madness" series, designed to be written late at night where my thoughts will be at their rawest form. I will be unapologetic. I will stand by my beliefs. These are not entries to read for those seeking shelter.

About Me

Well the thing I hate talking about most is me. But... I guess I can share a few details with you.

This will be the first entry of Midnight Madness - Entry "0" if you will, programmers will understand. I started this website one restless night at 3 in the morning after I spent the whole day coding... maybe I guess I just couldn't get enough? As I laid awake in the darkness just staring at the ceiling, I looked torwards the future and saw nothing. Absolutely nothing. I had no clue what lay ahead, and as someone who finds comfort through knowledge, this lack of foresight disturbed me greatly. Where could I find answers though? I realized that the questions about my future could only be answered by myself. What I do is ultimately decided by solely me.
